Pawsitive a website based on stray animal adoption and their care


1. INTRODUCTION

Pawsitive aims to create loving homes for stray animals and foster meaningful bonds between animals and humans, free of cost. We believe that every pet deserves a happy, healthy life, enriching the lives of their owners. Notably, our project is proudly developed with zero budget.

1.1 Significance of the Project

Our project addresses the need to provide a new lease on life to stray animals while combating loneliness and depression in individuals. Pets have the remarkable ability to bring joy and companionship during difficult times, making our endeavor significant.

2. OBJECTIVE

* Facilitate the adoption of stray animals, including dogs, cats, and cows, by providing a digital platform connecting potential owners with new furry friends.
* Serve as a comprehensive resource hub for pet owners, offering tips, advice, and support on pet care and street/local breed pet adoption.
* Mitigate the risks of road accidents and struggles for survival faced by stray animals by providing them with a safe and caring environment.

3.METHODOLOGY

Our methodology for developing the Pawsitive website primarily revolves around utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript within Visual Studio Code for coding purposes. For design, we heavily relied on Figma, an intuitive design tool known for its collaborative features and ease of use. To enhance user interaction and engagement, we incorporated AI functionalities, including ChatGPT, Blackbox, and BARD by Google. These AI technologies empower the website with intelligent features, enabling seamless communication and providing users with valuable assistance throughout their journey on the platform.
Throughout the development process, we extensively utilized online resources such as Google and Stack Overflow to address technical challenges, seek solutions, and broaden our understanding of web development principles. Additionally, we actively engaged with the Figma open-source community, leveraging its wealth of resources, templates, and design assets to enhance the visual appeal and usability of the website.
Furthermore, we embraced a continuous learning approach by leveraging educational platforms like YouTube. Through tutorials, walkthroughs, and instructional videos, we honed our skills in web development, design principles, and AI integration, ensuring that our website meets the highest standards of functionality and aesthetics.
By employing this methodology, we aimed to create a user-centric website that not only facilitates stray animal adoption but also promotes responsible pet ownership. Through the seamless integration of technology, design, and AI, we endeavored to deliver an enriching and impactful online experience for both users and their furry companions.
